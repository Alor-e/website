# Mahsa Afzali Arani

I am a software engineering masters student in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Concordia University under the supervision of [Dr. Emad Shihab](/members/emad-shihab/). She received her bachelor’s degree in bio-medical engineering from Azad University, Iran. Her work focuses on analysing data acquired through wearable devices in order to improve human activity recognition (HAR).

Mahsa’s research interests: bio-signal/ image processing, wearable devices, Human Machine Interface (HMI).
